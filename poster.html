Methodology
Schema: Dublin Core (simple) + local extensions (dcx_)
Risk framework: 6-dimension Likelihood × Impact scoring
Date encoding: EDTF / ISO 8601 (uuuu = unknown year)
Descriptions: bilingual (original Cantonese + English)
Key Findings
1. Custodial abandonment is the dominant preservation risk
2. Three records require mandatory ethical review (WFC-015, 016, 019)
3. Format obsolescence is the least severe risk category
4. The preservation crisis is hosting and custodianship, not file formats
